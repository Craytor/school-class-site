Evaluation

National and Local Weather Forecast, Hurricane, Radar and Report

  1. Referring to the section in the handout titled Purpose of graphics in web design, what do you feel is the purpose of the graphics on the page you selected?

    I feel that the purpose of the graphics on this webpage is to convey certain senarios in the sites news and also show what the current weather is. For example, the site has multiple news articles, and images are used to show what the article is talking about. Also, if you go to your location for the current weather, you can see an image of what it looks like outside which will then you will be able to visually see it.

  2. Referring to the section in the handout titled Guidelines for effective use of web graphics, how well does the page you selected meet these guidelines? Which guidelines does it do especially well on, if any? Which guidelines does it fail to meet?

    The website The Weather Channel meets the criteria (or guidelines) of a good effective use of web graphics. The file sizes are relativiely small to allow fast loading, the graphics are relative to the page content, they represent (or draw attention to) key objects within the page, and they are not overpowerful or distracting. The site uses image effectively, and the website benefits from doing so. Also, when displaying images, they use alt tags to display what the image is, and they also have a brief description under the image.
