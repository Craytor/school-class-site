Usability

Method

  • The site: Weather.com
  • Target Audience: People whom want to know the weather for different locations.
  • Tasks:
    • Find the weather for our current location.
    • How can you find information about carrers.
    • How are tornado seasons changing?

Results

  • Were subjects able to complete the tasks you assigned them? Yes - the subject was able to do all of the requests that I made for them with out any issues what-so-ever.
  • Was it easy for them? For the most part, it was indeed easy for them. The last question was a bit of an issue, but they were eventually able to figure it out.
  • How much time, or how many steps, did it take to complete the tasks? For the most part it only took a few minutes for them to figure out how to solve the situation.

Discussion

  • What did you learn from this study about the website? I learned that it is important to understand that everyone may go about certain things a diifferent way than you may.
  • Is the current design effective? Yes, the design works pretty well. They were able to easily navigate the site for instant results.
  • How might the site be designed differently so that users could more easily perform the representative tasks? Mostly for the last question, The Weather Channel should better format the way they show their "important" articles, and not their "junk" articles.
